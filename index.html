Who are we?

A group of friends from the North Florida area, we created TiDDY to fill a gap that we have noticed in the clothing industry for years. TiDDY is not defined by age, race or gender, but by what you do when you have the TiDDY logo on your shirt. Our goal is to make clothing that is comfortable and accessable to everyone, and to look good while doing it.
